## 💡 **Pricing Strategy**

### **Value-Based Pricing**
- **Enterprise Value**: Focus on ROI and business value
- **Competitive Positioning**: Premium pricing for premium features
- **Tiered Approach**: Multiple price points for different segments
- **Usage-Based**: Cloud offerings based on actual usage

### **Pricing Models**

#### **Subscription Model**
- **Predictable Revenue**: Monthly/annual recurring revenue
- **Customer Retention**: Higher lifetime value
- **Scalability**: Easy to scale with customer growth
- **Upsell Opportunities**: Natural progression to higher tiers

#### **Perpetual Licensing**
- **Large Enterprises**: Customers who prefer ownership
- **High-Value Deals**: Larger upfront revenue
- **Custom Deployments**: Specialized requirements
- **Long-Term Relationships**: Deeper customer engagement

#### **Usage-Based Pricing**
- **Cloud Native**: Aligns with cloud consumption patterns
- **Fair Pricing**: Pay for what you use
- **Scalable**: Grows with customer usage
- **Entry Barrier**: Lower initial cost
