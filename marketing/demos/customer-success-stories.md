## 🎥 **Production Guidelines**

### **Interview Structure**
- **Pre-Interview**: Technical briefing and questions review
- **On-Site**: Facility tour and infrastructure showcase
- **Interview**: Professional setup with good lighting and audio
- **Demo**: Live or recorded technical demonstration
- **B-Roll**: Additional footage for context and visual interest

### **Technical Requirements**
- **Video Quality**: 4K resolution, professional lighting
- **Audio Quality**: Lavalier microphones, minimal background noise
- **B-Roll**: Screen recordings, facility shots, team footage
- **Graphics**: Consistent branding and lower thirds
- **Music**: Professional background music appropriate for tone

### **Content Guidelines**
- **Authenticity**: Real stories, genuine experiences
- **Technical Depth**: Specific examples and metrics
- **Business Value**: Clear ROI and business impact
- **Human Element**: Team experiences and insights
- **Visual Variety**: Mix of interview, demo, and b-roll

### **Legal Considerations**
- **Release Forms**: Signed releases for all participants
- **Confidentiality**: No sensitive company data
- **Brand Guidelines**: Customer brand compliance
- **Approval Process**: Customer review and approval
- **Usage Rights**: Clear distribution and usage rights

- Video editing and color grading
- Graphics and animation

- Blog post and case study creation
- Social media promotion
