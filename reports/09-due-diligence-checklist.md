# Due Diligence Checklist - Seed/Series A Investor Onboarding

## 📋 **Due Diligence Overview**

### **Purpose**
This comprehensive due diligence checklist provides investors with a structured approach to evaluating the NixOS Gateway investment opportunity across all critical dimensions.

### **Scope**
- **Business Due Diligence**: Market, business model, competitive analysis
- **Technical Due Diligence**: Product, technology, architecture, IP
- **Financial Due Diligence**: Financials, projections, unit economics
- **Legal Due Diligence**: Corporate, contracts, compliance, IP
- **Team Due Diligence**: Leadership, team, culture, references

#### **Market Size & Growth**
- [ ] Verify TAM/SAM/SOM calculations with independent research
