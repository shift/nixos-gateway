### **Founding Story**
Founded in 2024 by a team of enterprise networking and open source experts who recognized the growing complexity of network infrastructure management. The founders saw an opportunity to combine the declarative power of NixOS with enterprise-grade networking needs.

- **2024 Q1**: Company incorporation and initial funding
